My Tyke and I

Kelly and Brenda's story.

Kelly and Brenda have worked together for 15 years in their previous salon and now are opening a small studio together. They have learned a lot together and have decided to bring their combined styles together to be the dream team needed in the industry they love. Many years of honing their cutting skills. Learning the tips and tricks of the most up to date styles for men and children. Learning the wants and needs of what parents want for their children. We will be offering an Unending amount of patience and understanding for children and their needs during a haircut experience. We understand the need to move with them and that children have fears that we have learned to work with. We have brought together the My tyke and I experience because nothing is more fun than the tradition of taking your child for that "my daddy and me" haircut.

Kelly Schlick

Co - Owner

Kelly Schlick has worked in a family centered salon since 2007, specializing mostly in men & boys haircuts. Kelly has kept up with the most popular trends by attending trainings and conferences throughout the years. Kelly grew up on a horse ranch and worked with children as she taught riding lessons, teaching her lots of patience. She is the youngest of 4 siblings, her brothers who were firefighters in their local towns & her sister who works with an amusement park for kids. Kelly has truly found her passion in working with children, but having a son of her own she has noticed that boys are growing up and want more of that grown up barber experience. This is something she hopes to bring to the community in opening up My Tyke and I.
